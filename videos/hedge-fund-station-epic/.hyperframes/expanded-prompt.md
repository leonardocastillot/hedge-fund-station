# Expanded Prompt: Hedge Fund Station Epic

## Style Block

Use the Hedge Fund Station brand tokens from `design.md`: background `#020408`,
surface `#060a14`, raised surface `#0e1424`, text `#f0f2f5`, accent red
`#ef4444`, warm accent `#f97316`, signal cyan `#22d3ee`, positive `#34d399`,
negative `#fb7185`, and warning `#f59e0b`. Typography uses Outfit for product
copy and JetBrains Mono for command/evidence surfaces.

## Rhythm Declaration

`hook-build-BUILD-PEAK-drift-secure-resolve` across 40 seconds.

## Global Rules

- Horizontal `1920x1080`, `40s`, 30fps target.
- Product-first: every scene includes a sanitized app surface.
- Captions are large, bottom anchored, and designed to carry the story without
  voiceover.
- Captions animate with cinematic entrance: zoom, blur, wipe, light sweep, and
  deterministic hard exit so every message lands cleanly.
- Audio is a single instrumental background song.
- Use red scan pulses for risk, cyan rails for market data, emerald gates for
  safety, and orange sparks for action.
- Use sanitized demo numbers and labels only.

## Beats

1. **Opening Shell, 0-4s**
   The requested `renders/primera.png` capture emerges from darkness like a
   command center booting. The product title lands large, while the image holds
   the real Hedge Fund Station surface beneath the title in a more frontal,
   readable position.

2. **Research OS, 4-10s**
   The requested `renders/backtesting.png` capture becomes the Backtesting
   Evidence scene. It shows trades, PnL curve, fees, validation gate, and an
   auditable ledger before Paper.

3. **Strategy Pipeline, 10-16s**
   The strategy board spreads into four columns: Backtesting, Audit, Paper,
   Blocked. Strategy cards show evidence gates, not hype.

4. **Market Intelligence, 16-22s**
   Hyperliquid radar and liquidation queue split the frame. Charts draw, pressure
   bars fill, and a "facts vs inference" divider separates source data from
   interpretation.

5. **Agents + Memory, 22-29s**
   The requested `renders/grafo.png` capture anchors the Strategy Memory Graph
   section. Callouts connect docs, backend logic, backtests, validation gates,
   paper ledgers, live readiness, and reusable lessons. The mission workbench
   shows guarded commands and operator approval.

6. **Paper + Live Monitor, 29-36s**
   Paper Lab, replay, review queue, and Live Trading readiness gates show the
   safety boundary. "Monitor only" and "human approval" are visually explicit.

7. **Resolution, 36-40s**
   The pipeline condenses into the full operating loop:
   Research -> Backtest -> Validate -> Paper -> Live. End on logo and the
   message that Live is the result of evidence, risk review, and approval.

## Negative Prompt

No real credentials, no private paths, no raw local endpoints, no exact current
PnL, no claim that live trading is active, no generic SaaS landing page, and no
decorative scene that lacks product substance.
