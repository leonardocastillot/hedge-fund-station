## Overview

Dark institutional product film for a hedge fund research operating system. The
visual language comes from the app's Obsidian Red theme: black glass, red alerts,
orange heat, cyan market data, emerald safety states, and monospace command
surfaces.

## Do's

- Keep the app shell visible as the main product signal.
- Use large readable labels and sanitized demo values.
- Make backend evidence, validation gates, paper review, and human approval feel
  central.
- Use cinematic camera movement, scan lines, data rails, and command pulses.

## Don'ts

- Do not imply live execution is enabled.
- Do not show local paths, credentials, endpoints, raw PnL, or private state.
- Do not use decorative UI that hides the product surfaces.
